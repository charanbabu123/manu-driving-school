Complete Guide

How to Get a Driving Licence
in Bangalore

A step-by-step guide to getting your driving licence — from application to road test. Manu Driving School assists you at every stage.

01
📝

Apply for Learner's Licence

Visit your nearest RTO or apply online at parivahan.gov.in. You need Aadhaar card, a passport photo, and approximately ₹200 fee. Manu Driving School assists with the complete application process including form filling and document preparation.

02
🚗

Complete Driving Training

Karnataka law requires minimum 30 hours of driving training before the licence test. At Manu Driving School, most students complete training in 4–5 weeks with certified instructors and dual-control cars that keep you safe throughout the learning process.

03
🎯

Driving Test at RTO

The RTO test includes a track test (figure-8, gradient, parallel parking) and a road test. Manu Driving School coaches you specifically for Bangalore RTO requirements and accompanies you on test day for support and confidence.
